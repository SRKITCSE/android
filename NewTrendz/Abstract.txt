NEW TRENDZZ is an android based application which stores the details of new accessories, devices, mobiles, tabs & so on that 
belongs to the user. Each user can upload photos of their accessories or products into “NEW TRENDZZ”. Each post contains the 
details of product (shop name, itemname, image, shopadress, shopphonenumber, feedback) which are entered by the user when 
they post it to the application. The post can be viewed by list of friends in our contacts who having the same application

This project uses MYSQL that allow you to maintain a free and easy access to organize database of profiles, product images. 
The Flexibility of the application is that we can easily gain the information about products that are used by our friends. 
Using this applicationsave tremendous amount of time in searching similar kind of products across various websites and retail shops.
