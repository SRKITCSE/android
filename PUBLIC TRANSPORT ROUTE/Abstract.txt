Public Transport Route application provides the facility to know bus routes, bus numbers,
and nearby bus stops. It also provides the bus details from source to destination along
with the fare and journey time. It also provides the facility to set alarm when travelling
from source to destination, when nearby destination then alarm rings; it is like giving
alertness to commuter. This application is not only useful for commuters who travel by
bus but also for others who are on foot, on bike or on car. It also provides the facility to
know whereabouts of the bus. The admin can login and insert the buses information.This
application is very useful, user-friendly, effective and informative for the commuters who
travel in the city.
