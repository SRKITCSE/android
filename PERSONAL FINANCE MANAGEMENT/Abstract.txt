

PERSONAL FINANCE MANAGEMENT APPLICATION is the perfect solution for managing our finances. we can organize our
 cash flow, finance, bank accounts in one place. This can help to track our progress, discover our savings, and thereby helps in
 saving the money.                                  
                                              
In present day life most of us forget our expenses due to busy schedules and work tensions. Due to this some people will literally
 note down all their transactions and expenses manually. So in order to overcome this application is used .This application helps 
in managing our daily expenses and transactions and helps in saving money.
